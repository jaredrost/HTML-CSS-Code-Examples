Clinics

Winter Hitting Clinics

(Small Groups)

Lefty's weekly hitting-specific Clinics will focus on a
player's approach to every at bat as well as the proper
stance and how to get the most power out of his or her swing.

Each clinic will meet for one hour once a week for four
weeks. These clinics are kept to small groups; space
is limited.

These Clinics will run from January until April

View our registration form here

Winter Pitching Clinics

(Small Groups)

During Lefty's weekly Pitching-Specific Clinics
players will work on everything pitching
including balance, how to use their legs, pitch
grips, arm angles and how to approach each
batter. Each clinic meets for an hour once a
week for four weeks.
These clinics are kept to small groups; space is limited.

These Clinics will run from January until April
